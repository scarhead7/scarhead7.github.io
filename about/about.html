About Me

Bio (Somewhat short, I know)

My name is Dallin Snell. My dad has been in the United States military for my entire life. Because of that, I haven't lived in one place longer than three to four years. I was born in Oklahoma, but most recently lived in Alabama and Utah. Even though I've lived all over the country, I claim Alabama as my home. (War Eagle!) I am currently studying Computer Science at Brigham Young University-Idaho in Rexburg, Idaho.

Interests

TV

I've had a revived interest in TV shows in the past two years or so. One show I'm really into is Game of Thrones (yes, I know, everybody is in to it too). I'm waiting (with bated breath) for the eighth season to come out; but who isn't, right? I also watch Ballers (finished the last season last night). As for non-HBO shows, I watch Burn Notice (the one with Jeffrey Donovan and Gabrielle Anwar) and Life (starring Damian Lewis and Adam Arkin).

I also enjoying going to the theater to watch new movies, but I'm a poor college student so that doesn't happen as often as I'd like. I did go and see Geostorm recently. It was decent. Not quite what I expected, but definitely a good watch. Flatlines was also good. It almost looked like the producers were trying for a scientific horror; so if you're easily scared, stay away. There was a guy in the theater who would scream at every intense part (Seriously, even if it wasn't scary and was just slightly intense, he would jump and yell. It was great.). Jumanji was fantastic! The actors did a phenomenal job while filming; however, I feel that Jack Black and Kevin Hart really made the movie. Definitely a must-see.
