Life can be tough sometimes. You can get beat down day in and day out. However, not all is darkness in life. There is a tremendous amount of good in the world. This pages chronicles a collection of words of wisdom from some amazing men and women who have inspired me when times were tough and all seemed dark. These men and women range from religious leaders to Christian rappers to corporate and world leaders and so forth. I include videos, quotes, song lyrics, and links to other pages. Enjoy and I hope this page can raise your spirits a bit (even if you feel like you don't need it).

Lecrae



Lecrae is a Christian rapper with a interesting past (look him up; he's pretty awesome!). He was born on October 9, 1979 in Houston, Texas. He co-founded Reach Records (probably the biggest Christian rap record company in existance) and also is a prominent member in 116 Clique, a Christian hip-hop group. Check out his website here. Also, for a more in-depth biography (and also to keep up-to-date on what he's doing), you can view his page on Reach Records here.

The video below is one of Lecrae's newest songs. Tori Kelly features with Lecrae in I'll Find You. Unfortunately, even though the video looks like it's there, you'll still have to go to YouTube to watch it. It's worth it though!

Flame



Marcus Tyrone Gray--born September 16, 1981 in St. Louis, Missouri--started his Christian rap career in his home town. Flame became his stage name in 2004 when he started with Cross Movement Records. In 2010, Flame started his own record company, Clear Sight Records. For a more detailed biography, you can go here.
