Life can be tough sometimes. You can get beat down day in and day out. However, not all is darkness in life. There is a tremendous amount of good in the world. This pages chronicles a collection of words of wisdom from some amazing men and women who have inspired me when times were tough and all seemed dark. These men and women range from religious leaders to corporate and world leaders and so forth. I have included videos, quotes, and links to other pages. Enjoy and I hope this page can raise your spirits a bit (even if you feel like you don't need it).
